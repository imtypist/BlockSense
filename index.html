BlockSense beta

We propose a blockchain-based crowd-sensing platform (BlockSense) to make up for the paucity of security in traditional crowd-sensing systems. Different from traditional crowd-sensing platforms, we introduce a new role, miner, to help requesters verify the sensing data quality.
